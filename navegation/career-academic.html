Education and Career

Academics

I completed my primary and secondary education in public schools. During high school, through considerable effort, I earned admission to the Instituto Federal de Educação, Ciência e Tecnologia Sul-Riograndense in Charqueadas, Rio Grande do Sul, Brazil. In addition to the standard curriculum, I studied subjects related to my technical course in Mechatronics. This experience not only provided access to high-quality education but also introduced me to the world of technology and the many opportunities it offers.
During this period, I took courses in robotics, electronics, digital electronics, automation, and programming, among others.
Currently, I am pursuing a degree in Systems Analysis and Development at UNINTER, with the goal of expanding my programming knowledge, enhancing my skills in web development (both frontend and backend), and eventually transitioning into mobile development.

Professional Experience

I began my professional journey as an intern at Gerdau in Charqueadas, RS, Brazil, as part of the mandatory practical training for my technical program. After graduation, I joined Icatu Seguros as a Young Apprentice. Approximately a year and a half later, I was promoted to a full-time position and currently work as a Commercial Support Assistant.
In this role, I provide support to our commercial partners regarding inquiries about Life Insurance, Capitalization, and Private Pension products. I am also responsible for producing monthly reports to monitor performance metrics and track service requests received from our partners.
Recently, I also started working as a freelance full-stack developer, contributing to projects using Node.js, TypeScript, and React.js for a company focused on sports analytics.
